On The Nature of God and Existence

God contains the set of all things, the existing and the non-existing. But that is only on one plane of reality. You have to go up to the next level and there God also is and includes the set of all ideas existing and non-existing, and so on vertically.

Existence itself is only one characteristic of God. The hidden face where he makes himself non-existent is a result of our own hiding ourselves from him by not talking and communicating with him.
