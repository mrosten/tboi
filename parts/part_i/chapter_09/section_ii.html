One class from each one of the main four categories should be learned each day. For instance: learn the Bible, then a class in halachah, then one in Talmud or science, and then one in Kabbalah. The Books which come from Beauty of Atzilut are Sipurai Maasiot [Crown], Tanya [Wisdom], Noam Elimelech [Understanding], Likutai Moharan, Sefer HaMidot [six middle sephirot] [Malchut]. In these right and left hand sephirot are located the roots of the prophets. In those middle three sephirot are located the roots of the sacred writings [like the psalms proverbs, etc].

The next diagram is a closeup of Formation of Royalty.

The middle three “pbk” [power, beauty, kindness] are the root of the three parts of the Mishna Seeds, Times and Women. The lower three are the roots of the three other parts Damages, Sacrifices, and Purity. By learning the mishnayot Seeds one draws the light of the name mlp la pla from the top four vessels into the vessel of kindness. By learning tractate Times, one draws from the light of the name myhla into the vessel of Power. The tractate Women draw the light of the name tvabe into the vessels of Beauty. By learning Damages, one draws the light of the name laa into the vessel of Victory. Learning the tractate Sacrifices one draws the light of the name myhla yhla hla laa into the vessel of Splendor. Purity draws the light of yds into the vessel of Foundation.

So, the entire Talmud is only the Talmud of the Nefesh [lowest level of the soul], not the spirit or the soul. The reason the Talmud is incomplete is that the souls of the Amoraim [rabbis of the Talmud] were from Beauty of Emanation...
